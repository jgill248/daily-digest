# AI Daily Digest — June 8, 2026 — Summary

- **Apple WWDC 2026: Siri rebuilt on Google Gemini, iOS gets AI model choice.** Apple unveiled a completely rebuilt Siri running on a custom 1.2-trillion-parameter Gemini model ($1B/year license), plus an Extensions system letting users swap between ChatGPT, Gemini, or Claude as their Apple Intelligence engine — the first OS-level multi-vendor AI marketplace. Tim Cook delivers the announcement as his final WWDC keynote before stepping down September 1.

- **Enterprise AI ROI data crystallizes: 5.1-month median payback, 95% of pilots fail.** BCG/Forrester report a 5.1-month median payback period on production AI agent deployments; Deloitte finds 65% of enterprises raised AI budgets in 2026; but Stanford finds 95% of pilots never produce measurable ROI — pointing to organizational change management, not model quality, as the true bottleneck.
